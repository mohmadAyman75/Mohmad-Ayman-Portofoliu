About Me

I’m Mohamed Ayman, deeply passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I started my journey in this field three years ago with great enthusiasm, and I decided to take it seriously by studying Data Science at Helwan University. From here, the story began.

I took many courses across different areas to clearly understand what I do and what I want to achieve, including Data Analysis, Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Large Language Models (LLMs). I also trained in these same fields at more than one place.

During this period, I focused on reading and learning more about the field and staying up to date with the latest developments through research papers, especially in Machine Learning by Dr. Andrew Ng and in Data Science by Dr. Cassie Kozyrkov.

In addition, I worked on building an audience and professional presence on platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub, and freelancing websites.

I wanted to share my story from the very beginning, and I’d be happy to work together. I’m ready — let’s talk.
